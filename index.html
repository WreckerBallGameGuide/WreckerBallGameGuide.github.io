The Basics

Hyper Scape is a 100 player PvP battle royale video game made by Ubisoft. The game is only playable on Xbox, PS4 and PC. In this game you can double jump and take no fall damage if you fall. The objective in this game is to be the last one standing out of the 100 players. In this game there is 2 ways to win to be the last one standing or to hold onto the crown for 45 seconds. like other battle royales there are guns to fight your opponent. What makes this game unique is a tool called a Hack. A Hack is a tool which gives you an advantage against your opponent. Once you are done downloading you spawn in a training arena. A robot will appear in front of you she will explain a bit of the game. After that you will get to choose your champion. There will be eight diffrent champion its okay if you pick a champion you don't like they will all be in your locker. After that you will spawn in a room which lets you experiment with diffrent weapons and Hacks. Once your done you will be teloported to the hub where you can play solos, squads or Fraction wars. You can also look at the news and updates, customise your locker, check out the battle pass and challenges, and visit the market place in the hub to purchase items. If you can you can urge your friends to play with you. When you load into a match you will be spawn into a island where you have to wait for players to load. On the isalnd you can do some parkour and play around. Once everyone is done loading you will be sent onto the main island where everyone starts at the same location. Once you start droping you can travel around the island. Hyper Scape has paired with Prime Gaming to stream the game all over. Prime allows viewers to choose diffrent events. Instead of diffrent rarity and chests you fuse weapons and there are crates all over the map.


On the main island there are 8 main locations to land and 24 other locations. If you land at the main locations grt inside the building for loot. When you land in other place look for buildings with orange barrier that you can break. Don't land at buildings with no orange barriers that indicates that the building has no loot and the doors are locked. In Hyper Scape instead of a storm locations collapse and every second your in a collapse location a bit of your health drains. When all but one location has collpase a crown spawns. You can either eliminate everyone or hold onto the crown for 45 seconds to win. In the game you can only hold 2 hacks and weapons

Hacks

There are 11 diffrent types of Hacks in Hyper Scape

Invisibility

Invisibility is a Hack that allows you to become invisible for a short duration of time. It is great for running away from enemies.

Mine

Mine is a mine that you can place anywhere to explode people. This Hack is the worst to die to because you feel stupid that you ran into a mine but it feels good seeing enemies running into your mine.

Slam

Slam shoots you up in the air and comes smashing down to the ground like a rocket. Its great for rushing and getting away in a sticky situations. When you come bomming down it creates a little are that does splash damage.

Heal

Heal spawns a med area which slowly heals you and your teamates. It most effective when the Wall Hack is blocking shots.

Ball

Ball transforms you into a very very very bouncy ball that can bounce over tall buildings. The ball lasts very long but can be broken very easily.

Teleport

Teleport zooms you to a short distance. teloport, teloports you to the way you are facing.

Armour

Armour allows you to become invincible for a while.

Magnet

Magnet acttracts players towards yourself. Its perfect to use with the mine hack.

Reveal

Reveal is a hack that pins you enemies nearby you. When your in a house and you hear an opponent you can use reveal to ping them.

Shockwave

Shockwave is a hack that launches you in the air. Anyone near will take damage.
